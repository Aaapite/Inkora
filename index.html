Our Story

Craft over convenience

"A journal should age with you — not against you."

Inkora began in a small workshop in Kuala Lumpur, born from the belief that the act of writing deserves a worthy vessel. Every journal we make starts as a single piece of ethically sourced leather, cut and shaped entirely by hand.

We don't rush. We don't mass-produce. We make things slowly, on purpose — because objects made with intention carry it forward into everything you write.
